Dots Calculator
Dots are a measurement of a powerlifter's competitiveness. They are calculated using coefficients based on a lifter's sex and weight class.
Assuming fully-tested and raw lifting:
350 dots = Top 33%
400 dots = Top 13%
450 dots = Top 3%
500 dots = Top 0.4%

Jeff Nippard Strength Level Calculator
Jeff Nippard released a video discussing his strength level standards, calculated from a lifter's 1-rep-max (1RM) strength for each of the squat, bench, and deadlift in proportion to their bodyweight.
Inputting each lift individually will additionally display your strength level per lift.
